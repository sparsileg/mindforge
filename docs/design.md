Here are some enhancements that could improve the learning experience:

# Study Experience

    * Study statistics per deck - Show accuracy rate, average time per
      card, and mastery level for each deck so users can identify problem
      areas. 
    * Cloze deletions - Support fill-in-the-blank style cards where parts
      of the answer are hidden. Format: "The capital of France is
      {{Paris}}" becomes "The capital of France is _____"

# Spaced Repetition Improvements

    * Due date visibility - Show when cards are next due in the preview
      screen, helping users understand their review schedule. 
    * Cramming mode - Temporarily bypass the spaced repetition algorithm
      for exam prep, studying all cards regardless of schedule. 
    * Lapse handling - When cards are repeatedly failed, flag them for
      special attention or move to a "problem cards" collection. 

# Usability

    * Deck duplication - Clone existing decks to create variants or backups
      before making changes. 
    * Undo last action - Simple undo for accidental deletions or edits.

# Separate "study export" vs "full backup"

    * Study export: Only selected decks + statistics (for Android use)
    * Full backup: Everything (for disaster recovery)
    * Study export imports would merge, full backup would replace

You'd have:

    * "Create Full Backup" (what you have now - replaces everything on import)
    * "Export for Study" (select specific decks, merges statistics on
      import)
